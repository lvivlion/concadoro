Our Family Story

A Tradition of Taste in Plum Borough

Since 1976, Conca D'oro Pizzeria has been a cornerstone of the Pittsburgh community. What started as a small family dream in Plum, PA has blossomed into a local favorite, built on the foundations of authentic recipes, fresh ingredients, and a passion for bringing people together over a great meal.

We're proud to be a family-owned and operated establishment right here in the Pittsburgh area. Every pizza we craft, every hoagie we build, is a piece of our heritage. From our famous Pan Pizza to our Gourmet Hand-Tossed creations, we pour our hearts into our food. Thank you for being a part of our story.
